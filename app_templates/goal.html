የተግባር ግቦች (Goals)

  • የቤት ውስጥ አገልጋይነትን፣ ብዝበዛን፣ ጥቃትን እና ዘመናዊ ባርነትንለማጥፋት በሚደረገው ሀገራዊ፣ አህጉራዊ እና ዓለም ዓቀፋዊ ርብርብ የበኩላችንን አሥተዋጽኦ ማድረግ ነው።.

Kaayyolee (Goals)

  • Hojjettummaa mana keessaa seeraan alaa, miidhaa fi gabrummaa ammayyaa hambisuuf akka biyyaaf Addunyaatti tattaaffii godhaman irratti gahee keenya bahachuu.

Strategic Goals

  • Returnee domestic workers Local domestic workers Potential migrants/youth Child domestic workers Women, children, and girls who have experienced gender-based violence
